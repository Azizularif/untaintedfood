9 Foods Which Rejuvenate The Liver

The liver is an indispensable organ in the human body. It is responsible for keeping the blood clear of infections as well as maintaining the body temperature. However, certain diseases or conditions cause damage to the liver and lower its effectiveness. It is therefore essential to rejuvenate the liver by consuming healthy and energy giving foods. Most of these infections are caused by people’s behaviors such as alcoholism or poor dieting and unregulated use of medical drugs. Since dangerous liver conditions such as fatty liver have been increasingly reported. Many people are eager to find solutions on how to flush the kidneys and liver, how to improve liver function as well as the best fatty liver diet. The best way to keep the liver functioning is by having a healthy diet every day. Contrary to what people may think, a proper diet does not have to be too expensive. Having a considerable amount of fruits and vegetables in a day is much best for the liver. This is unlike consuming fried foods on a daily basis which increase the fat content in the body. The following is a discussion of cleansing foods that when incorporated in one’s diet will create a significant improvement on the functioning of the liver.

Garlic

A small amount of Garlic is capable of activating enzymes that help the body get rid of toxins. Consuming a reasonable amount of this pungent white bulb provides enough allicin and selenium. These natural compounds that aid to rejuvenate the liver as they cleanse it free from all toxins.

Citrus fruits

Citrus fruits like oranges and limes are rich in vitamin C and antioxidants which support the cleansing abilities of the liver. A small glass of fresh juice from such fruits enhances production of detoxification enzymes by the liver. These enzymes aid in getting rid of toxins such as carcinogens.

Beets and Carrots

Plant- flavonoids, and beta-carotene found in Beets and Carrots play a significant role in the general functioning of the liver. Eating carrots and beets on a regular basis enhances the operation of the liver.

Green tea

Antioxidants known as catechins compounds assist in the functioning of the liver. These antioxidants are found in green tea. Including green tea and not green tea extract is beneficial to the operation of the liver.

Green vegetables

Leafy green vegetables are known to be one of the best foods when it comes to cleansing the liver. Whether consumed raw or cooked, the chlorophyll rich leafy vegetables soak up environmental toxins from the blood. The green leafy vegetables offer protection to the liver by neutralizing heavy metals, chemicals, and pesticides in the bloodstream.

Apples

The high amounts of pectin in Apples are responsible for eliminating toxins from the digestive tract. In return, this makes it easier for the liver to get rid of toxins from the blood as the load is reduced.

Organic Oils

When used in regulated measures, cold pressed organic oils such as olive oil to provide a lipid base that absorbs toxins in the body. The burden of getting rid of toxins by the liver is therefore reduced making the liver function correctly.

Avocados.

The nutrients found in Avocados aid the Production of Glutathione, a compound that helps the liver get rid of harmful wastes. Consuming alternative grains such as millet plays a significant role to rejuvenate the liver and restore its optimum working capacity.

Grains

Grains that contain gluten only increase the sensitivity of the liver. People who experience sensitivity to gluten are likely to suffer abnormal liver enzymes. Enzyme production in the liver is supported by glucosinolate, a component found in Cruciferous vegetables such as Broccoli and cauliflower. These enzymes flush out toxins from the body and also reduce the risk of cancerous infections. Vitamin C helps the body synthesize toxic materials into substances that can be absorbed by water. Vitamin C is found in Lemons and Limes. Consuming lemons and limes increases the functioning of the liver. The amino acid arginine found in walnuts helps in detoxification of ammonia by the liver. Also, walnuts are rich in omega-3 fatty acids which aid in cleansing the liver. Consuming a reasonable amount of walnuts will result in a rejuvenation of the liver.

Vegetables

Just like Broccoli and cauliflower, Cabbage stimulates enzymes that help in detoxification and elimination of toxins so as to rejuvenate the liver. Turmeric helps the liver enzymes to detoxify and get rid of toxins. Consuming reasonable amounts of turmeric on a regular basis enhances the functioning of the liver.

Water.

Lastly, water is an essential part of the diet. All wastes within the body exist in solution form, and for them to be flushed out, water is needed. Taking sufficient water on daily basis boosts the functioning of the liver.
