How To Lose Hip Fat Fast For Women – Experts Recommend Doing This..

Keep Fit

Hip fat for some reason, tend to accumulate in the thighs and especially around the hips in both men and women, but mostly in Women. Hormones, genetics & unhealthy lifestyles are the biggest contributors of fat storage in such areas. When trying to lose fat in any part of your body, one has to seek a wholesome approach. Spot checking a certain part for fat loss is not entirely effective. You can accomplish fat loss on the hips by combining three things: proper diet, exercise and some lifestyle change. Dietary Interventions. If you have accumulated some fat, taking fewer calories in relation to what your body burns can help you reduce your fat reserves in the body. Then aim is to reduce the calorie intake so as to trigger your body in using the already accumulated fats. Below are some good dietary foods that can assist in losing hip fat. 1). Hydration. First and foremost, always ensure you are always hydrated. Often drink 3 to 4-liters of water daily. Water tends to flash out harmful toxins & carries nutrients to the cells, in addition to offering a moist environment for tissue development. Tips to hydrate to stimulate fat loss: – Squeeze lime into your drinking water to stimulate the fat loss process. – Avoid sugar laden drinks. – Take a daily dose of green tea as it is an excellent antioxidant with minimal calories. – Drink a glass of tea or water before meals to induce satiety. It stimulates metabolism and suppresses hunger. 2). Sweet potatoes. Try integrating sweet potatoes to most of your meals. They have a lower glycemic index compared to most foods like pasta and bread. High glycemic index foods tend to trigger hormone levels up, hence a hip fat accumulation. 3). Fruits. Fruits like oranges pineapples mangoes and etc. can assist you get smaller and leaner hips. 3). Whole Eggs. Whole eggs are rich in protein, some healthy fats, & will make you feel full. 4). Leafy Greens. Such greens include spinach, kales, swiss chards, and others. They possess several properties which make them awesome for a fat loss diet. They have low carbohydrates and calories but loaded with fiber. Taking leafy greens is a perfect way of increase your meal volumes, without increasing calories. 5). Fish. Oily fish like Tuna and Salmon are very healthy. It also keeps you full for longer periods with relatively few calories. They are loaded with healthy fats, protein, and many sorts of vital nutrients. 6). Cruciferous Vegetables. These vegetables include cauliflower, cabbage, brussels sprouts and broccoli. They are rich in fiber and incredibly fulfilling. This veggies also contain good amounts of protein. 7). Legumes and Beans. Most legumes and beans tend to be very beneficial in the fat loss process. This includes kidney beans, black beans, and lentils. They are rich in fiber and protein, which are vital nutrients that can lead to satiety. Resistant starch can also be sourced from such foods. 8). Cottage Cheese. Dairy foods are often rich in protein. Leading amongst them is cottage cheese. It’s mostly just protein having very little fat and carbohydrate. It’s also very satiating, giving a feeling of being full with less amounts of calories. 9). Avocados. Avocado is a unique fruit. While most fruits are rich in carbs, avocados packs healthy fats. Avocados are particularly rich in monounstoleic acid, similar to that found in olive oils 10). Apple Cider Vinegar. This is very popular with the natural health community. It’s often used in vinaigrettes or dressing. Other people dilute and drink with water. Apple cider vinegar is useful in your fat loss process. 11). Some Whole Grains. Some whole grains like quinoa, oats and brown rice are rich in fiber and contain decent amounts of protein. Oats are packed with soluble fibers and beta-glucans that increase satiety & improve metabolism. 12). Chili Pepper. Taking chili peppers can be good on a fat loss diet. They have a component called capsaicin, that is known to help in reducing appetite, increase fat burning and reduce hip fat accumulation. The substance has been incorporated into supplements for weight loss. 13). Chia Seeds. These seeds are considered to be amongst the most nutritious foods. They pack 12 grams of carbohydrates-per-ounce, 11 of which are fiber. This makes the seeds a low carb friendly food, & among the best fiber sources. 14). Coconut Oil. Coconut oil is rich in fatty acids called Medium Chain Triglycerides. They are known to boost satiety as well as increasing the calorie burning rate. 15). Full fat Yogurt. Yogurt contains a probiotic bacteria that improves the function of the gut. A good gut helps protect against leptin resistance, the main trigger of obesity. Foods To Avoid. Do away with unhealthy foods like ice cream, pastries, cakes and candies which are incredibly high in sugar. If you crave for them too much, then limit the intake quantities. Exercises to Lose Hip Fat. Most women can not lose hip fat by maintaining a healthy diet alone. Exercises are critical too in assisting with daily calorie burn in order to create a weight loss deficit. For a proper hip fat loss, aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate intensity exercise per day. Do the following exercises 3 times per session, moving as fast as possible between the exercises. Do the routine at least 3 times a weekly. 1). Normal Squats. This exercise works your butt, thighs, and above all the hips. An overall fat loss, will help with toning of your hips. How Many: 8-12. Reps: 10-20 How to: – Start off by putting your feet apart, placing your hands on your chest. – While diverting your weight to the heels squat down as if trying to sit downs. Push the buttocks behind you & go down until the thighs are parallel to the floor below. – Pause at this position and then slowly rise back to your original position. 2). Lunges. This is a nice exercise to assist in developing muscles around the hips and thighs. How Many: 8-12 Reps 10-20 How to: – Start by standing with feet apart & place your hands on the hips. – Step forward like 0.5 M with one foot keeping the toes pointed forward. Drop the back knee & bend the front knee in a slow movement. – Drop until the front thigh is parallel to the ground below. – Using the front thigh, push your body back to the original position. – Switch legs & repeat. 3). Hip Raises. This exercise specifically targets the muscles in the thighs and hips. It is perfect for toning the outer part of the hips. How Many: 8-12 Reps: 7-10 How to: – Lay down on your side. Rest the head on your hand and the upper hand on the hip. – Keeping the leg straight, raise the top leg. Lower it back to the original position slowly. – Switch sides & repeat with the other leg. 4). Bridge Exercise. Best known as a back exercise, it can also work your hips and thighs for a good tone. How Many: 8-12 Reps: 8-20 How to: – Lay down & face the ceiling. Bend the knees at a 90-degree angle. Rest the arms on the sides. – Pressing through the buttocks, raise the hips to the air until the body is declining straight from knees to head. – Pause for a while before slowly going back to the original position 5). Plié Squat. Improve mobility and strength by getting relatively low in the plié squats. How Many: 8-12 Reps: 10-20 How to: – Stand tall, feet positioned wider than shoulder width, toes pointed out. Keep the back straight and the weight on your heels. – Work your thighs and glutes as you lower to a deep squat. The thighs should be parallel to the ground below. – Powering with your heels, pushing back to the original position. 6). Lateral Step Out Squat. This exercise will fire up the thighs, glutes and hips. You can put a resistance band around the ankles to increase resistance. How Many: 8-12. Reps: 10-20. How to: – Stand up with the feet shoulder width apart. Keep the toes pointed forward & lower into a normal squat. – Hold the squat position and take 2-3 steps to the left. – Pause, then take 2-3 similar side steps to the right. 7). Plank Leg Lift. This exercise works the whole lower body while also toning up the shoulders and core. How Many: 8-12. Reps: 10-20. How to: – Get to a high plank position, hands positioned under the shoulders, butt down. – Squeeze the left glute to lift the left leg 2-inches off the ground, keeping the leg straight. – Tap the left leg out towards the side, then return to the original position. – Switch legs and repeat. 8). Fire Hydrant. This mobility focused move is better placed as a staple for your dynamic warm ups, it’s a killer hips shaper. How Many: 8-12 Reps: 10-20. How to: – Position yourself in a tabletop position. Work the abs by pulling the belly towards the spine. – Keeping the hips pointed to the ground & legs bent to a 90 degree angle, raise the left knee to the side. – Hold at the top, & then return to original position. – Switch legs & repeat. 9). Lying Leg Lift. This exercise will work the butt, hips and the whole upper leg in general. How Many: 8-12. Reps 10-20. How to: – Lie on your stomach, with the forehead resting on the hands – Squeeze the left glute, to lift the left leg & thigh as high as you can. Keep the leg straight. – Hold at the top, & then lower it down – Switch legs & repeat. 10). Standing Side Kick. This exercise targets the inner thighs, quadriceps, and outer hips. How Many: 8-12 Reps 10-20 How to: – With the feet hip wide apart & hands on the hips, slowly extend the right leg to your side at in three full counts. – Keep the inner-thigh parallel to the ground. – Pause for one count, & then take three counts to lower. – Switch legs & repeat. 11). Side Jump. The exercise targets the glutes, hamstrings, and the outer hips. How Many: 8-13 Reps: 10-20 How To: – Standing with your arms on hips, hop three feet to the left, landing on the left foot with the left knee slightly bent. – Switch legs. – Repeat to the right & continue alternating for 10-20 times on each side. Lifestyle Change. Above all, to accomplish the hip fat loss, one needs to evaluate and make some changes to her lifestyle. Lifestyle Tips: 1). Proper Sleep. Getting proper sleep can assist in your weight loss process. Yes i mean it. How? Lack of proper sleep triggers production of a hormones known as leptin and ghrelin, which stimulates appetite. With a much greater appetite, one can be tempted to consume extra calories that may be harmful to your fat loss process. 2). Rest. Taking short naps after exercises can also assist in fat loss from the hips. Exercises stimulates cells & enhances metabolism. Resting allows the ligaments and muscles to repair and heal. 3). Eat Dinner Early. Taking dinner right before bed converts the meal into fat directly because in sleep we are doing nothing to expend any calories. Plan to take dinner at least 4 hours before bed. 4). Do not Starve Yourself. Starving often slows down metabolism as the body prepares for the next intake. This causes storage of fats especially in problematic areas like hips. 5). Eat Small Meals. It’s recommended to take smaller portions of meals more often. Don’t skip all meals & indulge in a feast right before bedtime. This will lead to accumulation of fat. Conclusion. In women, hips are a common place for storage of excess fat. While it is tempting to “spot treat” only that part, it isn’t a feasible goal. An overall fat loss is the key to reducing and burning fat of any part of the body. With a whole body fat reduction and weight loss, you will notice a loss of fat stored in the hips area. For a comprehensive outcome, try the above expert approach to losing hip fat fast.
