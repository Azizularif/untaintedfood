10 Best Home Remedies for Melasma

Melasma is a common skin condition that affects millions of people, the majority being women. Melasma causes brown-dark gray patches to appear on the skin. Several factors can trigger this condition, for instance, hormonal changes, toxins and taking birth control pills. But the primary cause is exposure to sunlight which leads to excess melanin production. Melasma appears on the cheeks, forehead, nose, and chin. If you prefer natural cures over chemical medications to melasma here are some suggestions. It is important to distinguish between melasma and hyperpigmentation which is an area on the skin that appears darker than the surrounding skin. 10 Melasma Natural Remedies

1. Lemon Juice

Lemon juice has citric acid which when applied to the face can help in treating melasma. It works by lightening the brown-gray patches on your face. Squeeze the juice and dilute it with warm water, apply on the face for some time then wash it off. Three Lemons one cut in half, treatment for melasma

2. Turmeric

Turmeric has medicinal properties for curing skin diseases. The ingredient cumin in turmeric has antioxidant and skin lightening properties. It reduces melanin pigmentation and flushes out toxins. It is a popular treatment for skin and hair problems. The vinegar has acetic acid which bleaches the spots and leaves your skin healthy and glowing.
