10 Amazing Health Benefits of Chia Seeds


Keep Fit

Chia seeds are one of the most common superfoods in every health-conscious community. When prepared correctly, the chia seeds are easy to digest, and it is also a beneficial ingredient that easily complements different recipes. Besides, chia health benefits are plentiful. Being rich in a variety of rich nutrients, regular consumption of chia seeds can boost your health in many ways. Some of the most important benefits include:

1. Skin and aging

Chia seeds have a high concentration of antioxidants. These antioxidants speed up the repair systems of your skin as well as preventing further damage. Consuming chia seeds can also prevent premature aging of your skin due to free radical damage and inflammation.

2. Digestive health

Chia seeds are high in fiber, giving you almost 11 grams in each ounce. One serving will offer you the recommended intake of fiber per day. Fiber also improves the ability of your body to balance the insulin levels. Seeds such as chia and flux are naturally blood sugar balancers due to their healthy fats and high fiber content. The gelatin-like substance created in your stomach once you consume chia seeds works as prebiotic which supports probiotics growth in your gut.

3. Heart health

The ability of chia seeds to regulate cholesterol, lower blood pressure and reverse inflammation makes it exceptionally beneficial for heart health.

4. Helps in the treatment of diabetes

Because chia seeds have high levels of fiber and alpha-linolenic, they assist in preventing metabolic disorders such as insulin resistance and excessive fat in your blood, which are the two critical factors in diabetes development.

5. Improving your metabolism and energy

Adding a chia seeds’ serving every day in your diet, you can burn belly fat and also boost your metabolism. Chia seeds also reduce the visceral tissue, a stomach fat tissue which affects your body’s metabolism and it’s also a factor in obesity.

6. Strong Bones

One ounce of chia has approximately 18% of the recommended amount of calcium per day. Calcium is essential in bone health as well as maintaining bone mass and strength. These seeds also contain boron, another essential nutrient for your bone health. Boron assists in metabolizing magnesium, phosphorous, manganese and calcium for healthy development of muscles and bones.

7. Builds muscle and promote weight loss

You can also use chia for weight loss and muscle building. Chia plant ranks among the best plant-based proteins, meaning you can use it to balance your blood sugar levels, burn fat and build lean muscle.

8. Dental health

Because chia seeds are full of zinc, vitamin A, phosphorous, and calcium, they are a superfood for your dental health. Phosphorous and vitamin A give you a healthy mouth and healthy teeth while calcium is the fundamental component of your tooth health and development.

9. Fight cervical and breast cancer

Chia seeds also contain alpha lipoic acid, an omega-3 fat acid that limits the development of cancer cells in cervical and breast cancers. Studies have shown that chia nutrition eradicates cancer cells without killing the healthy cells.

10. Easy to include in your diet

Chia seeds are incredibly easy to add to your diet. These seeds taste pretty bland, and you can incorporate them into almost anything. You don’t have to ground them like flax seeds making them easier to prepare. You can also eat them while raw, added to pudding and porridge, added to baked foods or soaked in juice.
